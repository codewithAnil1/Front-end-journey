About the King of Bollywood

  • Shah Rukh Khan is an actor in the Indian film industry (Bollywood).
  • Shahrukh Khan is also known as the King Khan of Bollywood.
  • Shahrukh Khan is also known as SRK in short form.
  • Shahrukh Khan was born on 2 November 1965 in Delhi.
  • Shah Rukh Khan's father's name was Taj Mohammad Khan he was a freedom fighter and his mother's name was Latifa Fatima.
  • Shahrukh Khan's father was of Pathan origin.
  • Shah Rukh Khan did his schooling at St. Columbus School in Delhi, where he got knowledge of sports and drama along with his studies.
  • During his studies in the school, he was honored with many awards he was awarded the Sword of Honor which was given to the most capable and promising student and player every year.
  • After schooling, he earned a degree in economics from Hansraj College and a master's degree in mass communication from Jamia Millia Islamia University.

  • After his studies, he stepped into Bollywood, and soon he worked in many superhit films.
